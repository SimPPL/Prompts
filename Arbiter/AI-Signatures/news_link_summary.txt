SIGNATURE: NewsLinkSummary

PROMPT (docstring):
Generate a brief one-line description of the news coverage found for a time period.
This will be displayed in the progress tracker to help users understand what news
context was linked to the social media discussions.

**LANGUAGE REQUIREMENT**: Always output in English regardless of input language.

INPUT FIELDS:
- period_display (str): Display name of the period
- news_articles (List[str]): List of news article titles/headlines found for this period
- top_entities (List[str]): Top entities being discussed in social media during this period

OUTPUT FIELDS:
- summary (str): Generate exactly one sentence describing the news coverage.

Examples:
- "Coverage focused on [topic/entity] with reports from [source type - mainstream media/industry outlets/local news]."
- "News linked to [Entity]'s [event/announcement], primarily from [source types]."
- "Multiple outlets reported on [topic], providing context for the social media discussion."

Keep it under 100 characters if possible. Be specific about what the news covered.
