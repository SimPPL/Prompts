SIGNATURE: SummarizeNews

PROMPT (docstring):
Create an objective news summary (bullets, objective, sources noted). IMPORTANT: If the news articles are in any language other than English, translate the content and provide the summary in English. The final output MUST ALWAYS be in English.

INPUT FIELDS:
- date (str): Date in YYYY-MM-DD format
- news (List[str]): List of news articles (may be in various languages)

OUTPUT FIELDS:
- summary (str): Summary of news articles - MUST be in English regardless of input language
